About

Where geospatial science meets disaster governance.

I am a GIS Analyst and Disaster Management professional with proven ability in technical solutioning, proposal development, and client engagement for climate resilience and disaster risk management projects. I combine strong technical expertise in geospatial analytics with strategic thinking to design and pitch scalable, data-driven solutions.

Embedded in Maharashtra's State Disaster Management Authority (SDMA) PMU through Palladium Consulting India Pvt. Ltd., I design and operate the GeoDSS — Geospatial Decision Support System — integrating multi-hazard, socio-economic, and real-time datasets for operational decision support at the State Emergency Operations Centre (SEOC).

My work spans the full spectrum from satellite data processing and climate model analysis (CMIP6, ERA5) to CM-level executive briefings and NDMA project pre-feasibility reports. I facilitate interagency coordination across DM R&R, IMD, INCOIS, IITM Pune, and development partners to translate complex geospatial outputs into actionable policy.
